# Proposal 5: The Eye

## Concept

An abstract eye/lens shape that represents the agent "watching" Linear for new issues and "seeing" the status changes. The form is reduced to two clean curves (upper and lower lid) framing a central circle. It's both an eye and a status indicator — the agent is always watching, always aware.

## Visual Description

- **Form**: Two opposing arcs (concave and convex) forming an almond/eye shape, with a small filled circle at the center. The upper arc is slightly thicker than the lower, creating subtle hierarchy
- **Color palette**: Dark graphite (#1f2937) for the eye outline, crisp cyan (#06b6d4) for the center pupil/circle
- **Style**: Ultra-flat, no gradients, no highlights. The arcs have flat ends (cut off cleanly at the edges). The eye shape is slightly wider than tall (roughly 3:2 ratio)
- **Composition**: Centered within a rounded square. Generous padding around the eye shape — it occupies about 50% of the container
- **Details**: The center circle is positioned very slightly above the geometric center of the eye shape (human eyes have pupils slightly above center). This small detail makes it feel considered rather than mechanical

## Mood

Watchful, intelligent, calm. The cyan dot adds a sense of "active monitoring" without feeling aggressive.

## Keywords for Generation

abstract eye logo, minimal almond shape, two arcs framing circle, graphite and cyan, flat design, no gradients, premium tech icon, monitoring concept, status awareness, geometric precision, rounded square container
