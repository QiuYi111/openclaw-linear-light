# Proposal 3: Light Pulse

## Concept

Inspired by the "light" in the project name. A single, clean pulse or ripple emanating from a central node — representing the moment an issue triggers an agent session. The ripple suggests action, responsiveness, and the lightweight nature of the integration.

## Visual Description

- **Form**: A small filled circle at center, surrounded by 2-3 concentric broken circles (arcs rather than full rings). The arcs are not uniform — they're broken at different points, creating an asymmetric, dynamic feel
- **Color palette**: Slate gray (#475569) for the outer arcs, bright teal (#14b8a6) for the center circle and the innermost arc
- **Style**: Pure flat, no gradients. Line weight decreases outward (thick center, thin outer arc) to create depth without shadows
- **Composition**: Circular format. The pulse pattern is offset slightly from dead center (maybe 10-15% toward upper-right) to feel alive rather than static
- **Details**: The broken arcs have clean, flat ends (not rounded). The gaps between arc segments are intentional and varied

## Mood

Responsive, alive, lightweight. Conveys "something just happened" — the moment of webhook trigger.
