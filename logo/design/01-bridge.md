# Proposal 1: The Bridge

## Concept

A minimal bridge icon that abstractly represents the connection between Linear (project management) and the AI agent. The bridge is formed by two geometric shapes reaching toward each other with a connecting element — symbolizing the webhook-driven communication channel.

## Visual Description

- **Form**: Two parallel horizontal lines (representing the two systems) connected by a series of clean vertical strokes forming a simplified truss bridge
- **Color palette**: Deep navy (#1a1a2e) primary, with a single accent of electric indigo (#6366f1) on one connecting element
- **Style**: Ultra-flat, no gradients, no shadows. Geometric precision with slight asymmetry to feel modern rather than rigid
- **Composition**: Centered, generous whitespace, the bridge sits within a rounded-corner square container
- **Details**: One of the vertical connecting strokes is subtly different (thicker or accented) to suggest the AI agent as the active element in the bridge

## Mood

Connected, reliable, architectural. Feels like a well-engineered solution rather than a decorative logo.

## Keywords for Generation

flat logo, minimalist bridge icon, geometric truss, navy and indigo, abstract connection symbol, clean lines, no gradients, premium tech logo, rounded square container, whitespace-heavy
